Summary Activity Reports

NOTE: Activity reports do not contain Personally Identifiable Information (PII) because the activity reports are sent to clients via email. Any reports with PII information need to be Admin Reports that are accessed and printed securely via FireLight® Admin.
Spotlight Video: See the Reports Spotlight Feature Video for more information.

Summary Activity Reports are run on a scheduler through our automated activity reporting tool and do not appear in Admin. Each report can be sent in CSV or PDF format and is sent on an automated schedule to an organization email. A distribution email is best as it allows you to change your recipients as needed and allows the report scheduler to run efficiently.

The Summary Activity Reports have default data columns. Some configurations, such as optional columns, are available. The Summary Activity Reports provide summary data about transaction activity but do not have access to specific form data that is not stored outside of the encrypted form data.

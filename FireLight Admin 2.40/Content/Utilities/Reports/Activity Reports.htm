Activity Reports

Activity reports are run on a scheduler and do not appear in ® Admin.

NOTE: Activity reports do not contain Personally Identifiable Information (PII) because the activity reports are sent to client via email. Any reports with PII information need to be Admin Reports that are accessed and printed securely via FireLight Admin. See the Reports Spotlight Feature Video for more information.

Typical Activity Report Setup

Carrier

  • Daily – Detail, Failed, Summary, Purged
  • Weekly – Pending Purge
  • Monthly – Detail, Summary

Carriers with Multiple Distributors

  • Daily – Detail By Channel, Failed, Purged
  • Weekly – Pending Purge
  • Monthly – Detail By Channel

Distributors with Multiple Carriers

  • Daily – Detail, Failed, Submission Summary (TrnSummary)
  • Weekly – Pending Purge
  • Monthly – Detail

Activity Reports

The following topics contain reporting options available through our automated activity reporting tool. Each report can be sent in CSV or PDF format and is sent on an automated schedule to an organization email. A distribution email is best as it allows you to change your recipients as needed and allows the report scheduler to run efficiently. The activity reports have default data columns. Some configuration, such as optional columns, are available. The activity reports can provide summary and detailed data about transaction activity, but do not have access to specific form data that is not stored outside of the encrypted form data.

Reports

Spotlight Video: See the Reports Spotlight Feature Video for more information.

On the navigation bar, click Utilities/Reports to go to the Reports page. Use the Reports page to generate, view and save Admin reports.

NOTE: Submit a JIRA ticket to request Admin, Detailed Activity, Summary Activity, and Custom Reports for your organization.

Step through the following topics to learn about Admin, Detailed Activity, Summary, and Custom reports that you can order for your organization.

Admin Reports Find descriptions and samples of available Admin Reports on the Utilities/Reports page in FireLight Admin that you can request for your organization. Any reports with Personally Identifiable Information (PII) need to be Admin Reports that are accessed and printed securely via FireLight Admin.

Detailed Activity Reports Find descriptions and samples of available Detailed Activity Reports that are available through our automated activity reporting tool that you can request for your organization along with typical activity report setup.

Summary Activity Reports Find descriptions and samples of available Summary Activity Reports that are available through our automated activity reporting tool that you can request for your organization along with typical activity report setup.

Custom Reports Find descriptions and samples of available Custom Reports that are available through our automated activity reporting tool that you can request for your organization along with typical activity report setup.

See the Admin online Help for samples of Admin, Detailed Activity and Summary Activity reports.

Deployments

On the Home page or navigation bar, click Utilities/Deployments to go to the Deployments page.

Step through the following for an overview of environment and deployment terminology, the testing process and workflow, and to find instructions for deploying data and viewing deployment history.

Terminology Find descriptions of deployment environments and deployment terms.

Testing Process Find guidelines and options for testing various carrier and distributor configurations.

Deployment Workflow View a FireLight® deployment workflow diagram.

Deploy Data Find instructions for moving new or updated data from the UAT environment (where all of your development and your testing occurs) to the Staging environment and then to the Production environment using the Deployment tab on the Deployments page.

View Deployment History Find instructions for viewing deployment history using the History tab on the Deployments page.

Lock and Unlock Wizards for Editing

NOTE: See Locking of Wizards, Rule Sets and Rules in the Wizard Designer for locking information from within the Wizard Designer.

This feature prevents multiple administrative users from accessing and updating the same wizard at the same time.

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer > Wizard Library to open the Wizard Library page.
  2. Click the unlocked button in the wizards list at the top of the Wizard Library page to lock the wizard for editing and prevent access by other administrative users.

  3. When finished editing, click the locked button to unlock the wizard for editing by other users.

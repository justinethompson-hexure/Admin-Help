Go to the Wizard Designer

  1. On the Home page or navigation bar, click Designer/Wizard Library to open the Wizard Library page.

  2. Select a wizard in the grid.

    NOTE: To lock a wizard to prevent the wizard from being edited by another user while you are working on the wizard, locate the wizard in the grid at the top of the Wizard Library page, and click the button to lock the wizard. When finished editing the wizard, click the button to unlock the wizard.

  3. On the Wizard Detail tab, click Wizard Designer to open the wizard on the Wizard Designer page, where you add pages, containers, fields and rules to design the selected wizard.

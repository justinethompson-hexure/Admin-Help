Select a Form to Design

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Designer to go to the form Designer page.

  2. On the upper left, select a form name in the list.

    Or, click the search button next to the list to open a Form Search dialog. Type some characters that are in the form name and click Go. FireLight® searches for all forms with your entry in the form name. Click a form in the list to open the form.

  3. On the form Designer page, if the selected form contains multiple pages, select a page number in the list to go to a specific page, for example, Page 2.

  4. Click the left arrow button or right arrow button at the edge of the form to move to the previous or next form page.

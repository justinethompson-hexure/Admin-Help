Save a Form

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Designer.
  2. On the upper left of the Form Designer page, select a form in the list and make updates to the form.
  3. Click the SAVE button on the upper left of the Form Designer page to save any changes that were made to the form.
  5. If you do not click the SAVE button on the upper left of the Form Designer page before leaving the page, a Form Changes dialog appears. Click Yes to save form changes and exit the form, click No to discard changes and exit the form, or click Cancel to return to the form and continue editing.

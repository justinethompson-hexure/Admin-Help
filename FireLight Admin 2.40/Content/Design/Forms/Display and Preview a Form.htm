Display and Preview a Form

Use the Display Form feature to preview a form in a PDF viewer.

  1. On the upper left of the Form Designer page, select a form in the list.
  2. On the left under Input Control, click Display Form.

  3. If the form does not automatically open in a PDF viewer (e.g., Adobe Reader) on a separate window, click the form file name in the Notification bar at the bottom of the page to open the form.

  4. 3. By default, fields appear with a white background.

    Some input controls, such as labels, may not appear in the PDF viewer.

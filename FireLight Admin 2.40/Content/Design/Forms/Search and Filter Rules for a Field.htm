Search and Filter Rules for a Field

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Designer.
  2. On the upper left of the Form Designer page, select a form in the list.
  3. On the left under Input Control, click Rules Window

  4. On the left of the Rules Window in Filter Rules with Field, type a full or partial field Field name and click the search button.

    On the left, the search results show get/set range rules if the tag is affected by the range.

View Form History

The Form History dialog is where you view a history of form changes, including add new control, delete control, move control, resize control, change field properties, align controls, and set tab order. The form history is updated when changes are made and the form is saved.

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Designer.

  2. On the upper left of the Form Designer page, select a form in the list.
  3. On the upper left, click the Form History link to open the Form History dialog.

  4. Filter the form history by date range. Click in the Show history from field and select a start date in the calendar, click in the To field and select an end date in the calendar, and click the Go button.

    Filter the form history by a new date range. Click the Reset button to clear the dates and select a new date range.

  5. Sort by column in ascending and descending order. Click a column title to sort the form history by Action, Old Value, New Value, Action Date, or Login. Click the same column title again to sort the form history in the reverse order (ascending or descending order).
  6. Click the Prev button to move to the previous page or click the Next button to move to the next page of form history.
  7. Click the X on the upper right to close the Form History dialog.
  8. In Utilities, Reports page, Admin Reports, run the Admin History Report for Forms History Audit Report to view an audit history report for all form changes during a selected time frame for a single form or all form.

Lock and Unlock Forms for Editing

NOTE: See Locking of Forms, Rule Sets and Rules in the Form Designer for locking information from within the Form Designer.

This feature prevents multiple administrative users from accessing and updating the same form at the same time.

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library to open the Form Library page.
  2. Click the unlocked button in the forms list at the top of the Forms List page to lock the form for editing and prevent access by other administrative users.
  3. Click the locked button when finished to unlock the form for editing by other users.

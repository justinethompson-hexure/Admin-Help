Replace a Form

Use the Replace Form feature to replace an existing form with a new form (replacing form page images).

  • All field definitions and rules from the original form remain the same on the new form.
  • If the new form has fewer pages, the old form’s extra page controls are copied to the first page/last page of the new form.

NOTE: You can replace only one form at a time.

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library.
  2. Select a form to be replaced in the form grid.

  3. In the lower center of the Form Detail tab, click the Replace Form button.

  4. (Optional) If the new form was previously exported from , select the Use exported FireLight definitions checkbox to retain the form fields.

  5. Click the Add Files button.

  6. Select the new replacement form to upload and click the Open button.

  7. On the Upload Form page to the right of the replacement form name, click the Start button to upload the form.

  8. When the upload is complete, click the Back button at the bottom of the page to return to the Form Library page.

  9. On the Form Library page, add any information to the new replacement form (Edition, Label, States Approved, etc.) as applicable and click the Save button at the lower center of the Form Detail tab.

  10. (Optional) On the navigation bar, click Designer to go to the Designer page and view the new replacement form.

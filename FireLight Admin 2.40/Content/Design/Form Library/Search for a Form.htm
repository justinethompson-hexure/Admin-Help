Search for a Form

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library to go to the Form Library page.

  2. On the upper left, begin typing the name of a form in the text box and click the search button.

    The Form grid displays the filtered list.

  3. In the grid, click a form in the Form Name column to select the form for editing.

    Select the Exclude Expired checkbox to exclude expired forms from the form grid.

  4. To begin another search, click Clear to the right of the search box to clear the search text and then type another form name to locate in the grid.

Go to the Form in the Form Designer

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library.

  2. On the Form Library page, select a form in the forms grid.

    NOTE: To lock a form to prevent the form from being edited by another user while you are working on the form, locate the form in the grid at the top of the Form Library page, and click the button to lock the form. When finished editing the form, click the button to unlock the form.

  3. In the lower center of the Form Detail tab, click the Form Designer button.

    The selected form opens in the Form Designer.

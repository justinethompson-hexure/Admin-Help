View Form History

Use the Form History tab on the Form Library page to view the history of the selected form.

Select a Form

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library to open the Form Library page.

  2. On the Form Library page, begin typing a form name in the box on the upper left and click the search button to filter the list of forms by the form name.

  3. Select a form from the grid on the upper right.

    Select the Exclude Expired checkbox to exclude expired forms from the form grid.

  4. To begin another search, click Clear to the right of the search box to clear the search text and then type another form name to locate in the grid.

View Form History

  1. On the navigation bar, click Designer/Form Library.

  2. Select a form in the grid at the top of the Form Library page.

    See "Select a Form" on this page for details.

  3. On the lower left of the Form Library page, click the Form History tab to view the history of the selected form.

  4. In Utilities, Reports page, Admin Reports, run the Admin History Report for Form History Audit Report to view an audit history report for all form changes during a selected time frame for a single form or all forms.

NOTE: The system purges all form deployment and audit history that is more than one year old.

Slider

Purpose

Provides a horizontal slider that is used to select a value within a range.

  1. Drag a Slider control from the right pane to the wizard page.
  2. On the page, double-click the Slider to open the Slider Properties dialog.
  4. Enter the remaining field properties and click OK to save your changes.
  5. On the upper left, click SAVE WIZARD to save the wizard, which unlocks the wizard for editing by other users.

DatePicker

Purpose

Provides a calendar control that is used for selecting a date.

  1. Drag a DatePicker control from the right pane to the wizard page.
  2. On the page, double-click the DatePicker to open the DatePicker Properties dialog.
  4. Enter the remaining field properties and click OK to save your changes.
  5. On the upper left, click SAVE WIZARD to save the wizard, which unlocks the wizard for editing by other users.

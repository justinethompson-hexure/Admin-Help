Divider

Purpose

Displays a horizontal line that divides sections of the page.

  1. Drag a Divider control from the right pane to the wizard page.
  2. On the page, double-click the Divider to open the Divider Properties dialog.
  4. Enter the remaining field properties and click OK to save your changes.
  5. On the upper left, click SAVE WIZARD to save the wizard, which unlocks the wizard for editing by other users.

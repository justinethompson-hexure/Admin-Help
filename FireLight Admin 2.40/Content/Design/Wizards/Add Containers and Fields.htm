  4. On the upper left, click the SAVE WIZARD button to save the wizard.

    NOTE: When you save a wizard, if the wizard is not locked and another user has saved changes to the wizard since you began your work, a Wizard Changes dialog opens. Choose one of the following buttons on the dialog: Overwrite Changes to overwrite the changes that the other user made and update the wizard with your latest changes, Discard Changes to discard the changes that you made and open the last saved version of the wizard by the other user, or Cancel Save to close the dialog and return to the last active location in the wizard.

Containers

Containers are optional. They are used to group fields together and to assist with complex layouts. You can embed containers within containers if desired.

The Wizard page has two types of containers: Group and Panel. A Group strictly manages the containers and fields within the Group. A Panel is similar to a Group, but has additional properties, such as a section header and help text.

Do the following to add and manage containers in a wizard:

  • Add a container – click and drag a Group or Panel from the right pane onto the wizard surface.

  • Select an existing container – click the container. The container activates and an orange border appears around it.

  • Edit a container – either double-click the container or click it and click the Edit button on the toolbar above the container.
  • Copy a container – click the container and click the Copy button toolbar above the container. The copy is placed at the bottom of the current page. The copied container includes all embedded containers and fields.
  • Delete a container – click the container and click the Delete button toolbar above the container. This deletes the container and its contents, including all embedded containers and fields.

  • Change the size of a container – click the container and increase or decrease the size by clicking the less or greater button on the toolbar. You can also edit any container and change the width in the field properties (see details in the individual container types).

    NOTE: Containers, and fields embedded within a container that is being re-sized, are not adjusted.

Fields

Do the following to add and manage fields in a wizard.

  • Add a field – click and drag an item from the right pane onto the wizard surface.

  • Select an existing field – click the field. The field is activated and an orange border appears around it with toolbar at the top of the field.

  • Edit a field – double-click the field to open the field properties or click the field and click the Edit button on the toolbar above the field.
  • Copy a field – click the field and click the Copy button on the toolbar above the field. The copy is placed at the bottom of the current page.
  • Delete a field – click the field and click the Delete button on the toolbar above the field.
  • Change the size of a field – click the field and increase or decrease the size by clicking the less or greater button on the toolbar. You can also edit any field and change the width in the field properties (see details in the individual field types).

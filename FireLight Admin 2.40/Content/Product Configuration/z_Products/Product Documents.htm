Product Documents

On the navigation bar, click Product Config and click Products to go to the Products page. Use the Product Documents dialog to add, view, and remove product documents for the Quote activity.

  1. Click the Product Documents link to open the Product Documents dialog.
  2. In Document Type, select the type of document to upload (Underwriting Guidelines or Product Guide).
  3. Click Choose File. Browse to the document location on the PC or network, click the document, and click Open.

  4. Click Upload.

    The chosen file (Underwriting Guidelines or Product Guide) appears at the top of the dialog along with links to View or Remove the document.

  5. Upload the remaining document type if applicable.
  6. Close the dialog.
  7. At the bottom of the Products page, click Save Product Config.

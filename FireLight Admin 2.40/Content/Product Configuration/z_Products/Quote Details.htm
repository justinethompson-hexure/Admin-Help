Quote Details

On the Product Configuration/Products page, click the Quote Details link to open the Quote Details dialog. Use the Quote Detail dialog to add Quote Detail for a product. See Quote Activity in Features for instructions to set up the Quote activity.

Add Rates

  1. At the top of the Products page, select a product in the grid.
  2. On the lower left, click the Quote Details link to open the Quote Details dialog.
  3. On the upper left, click Upload to open the Upload File dialog.
  4. In Rate File Name, enter a name for the rate file. The name is for reference only.

  5. in Risk Class / Mapping, enter the risk class mappings that correspond to Column C 'Risk Class' in the rate file.

    For example, Risk Class 0 might map to Standard Smoker and Risk Class 1 to Preferred Smoker. Seven risk classes are available in Quote to map to your product risk classes. If any Risk Class does not apply, leave the default value of -1 (e.g., if Simplified Issue does not apply to the product, leave both options as -1). Values in the rate file must be positive numeric values.

  6. Click Browse to open the Choose File to Upload dialog, select a rate file to upload, and click Open.
  7. Click Upload.

  8. On the Rates tab, enter a Start Date for the uploaded rate file. It defaults to today’s date plus one day. When the rates are updated, upload replacement rates with a later start date to replace the existing rates. Be sure the start date is later than the active rate files.

  9. In the Jurisdiction column of the grid, click Edit to open the State / Approved list. By default, all states are approved, including Guam, Puerto Rico, and Virgin Islands. Clear the checkboxes for the states where the product is not approved and click Save.
  10. (Optional) You can change the Rate File Name.
  11. On the Rates tab, click Save.
  12. Repeat the process for all rate files. For example, you might have rates for all states but New York and separate rates for New York. In this example, upload two rate files—one for New York and one for all the other states.
  13. Delete a rate file: click the red x for file in the right column of the grid.
  14. Update rates: upload the new rate file and delete the exiting rate file.

Add Rules

  1. In the Quote Details dialog, click the Rules tab.
  2. Click Browse to open the Choose File to Upload dialog, click the applicable rules file (for example, click Quote Product Rules Upload Template.xlsm), and click Open.
  3. On the Rules tab, click Upload.
  4. Review the rules for accuracy. Pause the mouse over the Show option in the Jurisdictions and Table Ratings columns to review the list of options.
  5. Update rules: you must replace all of the rules. On the network or PC, open the Quote Product Rules Upload Template.xlsm file, make the required changes and save the file. On the Rules tab, click Choose File, click the name of the updated rules file and upload it again.

Add Configuration

  1. In the Quote Details dialog, click the Configuration tab.
  2. Add the correct Modal Factors for the product.
  3. Add the Annual Policy Fee for the product.
  4. Add the Age Calculation method for the product. Valid options are: Last, Nearest, and Next. This is used to calculate issue age for the quotes.
  5. Click Save.
  6. At the end of the Products page, click Save Product Config.

(Optional) Add the product documents for each Term Life product:

  1. At the top of the Products page, select a Term Life product in the grid.
  2. On the lower left of the Products page, click the Product Documents link to open the Product Documents dialog.
  3. In Document Type, select the type of document to upload (Underwriting Guidelines or Product Guide).
  4. Click Choose File. Browse to the document location on the PC or network, click the document, and click Open.

  5. Click Upload.

    The chosen file (Underwriting Guidelines or Product Guide) appears at the top of the dialog along with links to View or Remove the document.

  6. At the end of the Products page, click Save Product Config.

Products

On the navigation bar, click Product Config and click Products to go to the Products page. Use the Products page to add and manage products and product features and to view product history.

Edit a Product

Edit a Product

  1. On the navigation bar, click Product Config/Products.
  2. In the product list grid, click a product name.
  3. Below the grid, update the product information, including state approvals. See "Add a Product" on this page.
  4. Click the Save Product Config button to save your updates.
 Delete a Product

Delete a Product

  1. On the navigation bar, click Product Config/Products.
  2. In the product list grid, click the red X to the right of the product name and product type.

  3. In the confirmation dialog, click Yes to delete the product from the system. Click No to close the dialog without deleting the product.

 Product Features

Product Features

On the Product Configuration/Products page, click the Product Features link to open the Product Features dialog. Use the dialog add product features to be displayed in an informational pop-up message during product selection when creating an activity in . The informational icon next to a product becomes available when a product feature is added. The information is also available for term product quotes (see "Quote Details").
NOTE: The list of features can be edited by using the Branding feature, if enabled.

Add, Edit, or Delete Product Features

  1. On the Products page, click the Product Features link to open the Product Feature dialog.

  2. Select a feature from the list (e.g., select Company A.M. Best Rating).

  3. Type the feature description (rating) in the text box below the feature name and click the Save button.

    A feature preview appears on the right. Product information is cached to improve response times. The Display Preview will update the preview of the feature information after a 20-minute delay.

  4. (Optional) Edit a selected feature: Click in the text box below a feature name and type the corrected text.

  5. (Optional) Delete a selected feature: Click the red X on the right of the text box for the feature and click the Save button.

  6. When finished, click the X on the upper right to close the dialog.

 View Product History

View Product History

Spotlight Video: See the History for Packages, Products, Sales Agreements, and Admin History Report Spotlight Feature Video for more information.

  1. On the Products page, select a product in the Products grid at the top of the page.
  2. Click the Product History tab.
  3. View the selected product history in the applicable columns of the history grid: Action, Old Value, New Value, Action Date, and Login.
  4. In Utilities, Reports page, Admin Reports, run the Admin History Report for Product History Audit Report to view an audit history report for one or all products during a selected time frame.

NOTE: The system purges all product deployment and audit history that is more than one year old.

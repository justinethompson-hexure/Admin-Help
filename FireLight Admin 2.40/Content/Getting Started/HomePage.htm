Home Page and Navigation

Use the links on the left of the Home page to view recent release notes. Click the following links as applicable:

  • Open Admin Help link on the left of the Home page or the Help link on the navigation bar to open the Admin Help.

  • Open FireLight Developer Help to open the developer Help for API and Sign On Methods.

  • FireLight Production Support Portal to open a support ticket.

  • FL-Support@insurancetechnologies.com to send an email to request user access to the support portal.

  • FireLight Support Process Guide to open the FireLight support process guide.

Click the buttons on the right of the Home page or click the links on the top navigation bar on other pages to navigate Admin.

The Home page and navigation bar buttons are grouped into five major sections: Product Configuration, Designer, Organization Configuration, Utilities, and Support.

  • On the Home page, click a button under a major section to go to the page.
  • On the navigation bar, click the name of a major section and click a page name in the list to go to the page. Click Close on the lower left of the list to close the list without going to a page.

Click Log Off on the navigation bar to end the FireLight® session.

Reset or Forgot Your FireLight Admin Password

  1. Open a web browser and go to the FireLight® Admin URL.

  2. Click the Forgot Password link on the Login dialog.

  3. Type your FireLight Admin User Name and click Reset My Password.

    FireLight sends you an email with a link for resetting your password.

  4. Click the link in the email message.

  5. Type your new password in New Password and Confirm Password and click OK.

  6. Use your new password to log in to FireLight Admin.

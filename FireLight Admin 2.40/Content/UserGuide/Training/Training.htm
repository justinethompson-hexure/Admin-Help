Training [User Instructions]

FireLightÂ® training videos are available if your organization has implemented the Training feature. The Hexure provided training videos include audio.

NOTE: Training videos are available based on your current location in FireLight. If no training videos are applicable for your location in FireLight, Training is not available on the navigation bar.

View Training Videos

  1. On the navigation bar, click Training.

    For example, the Training button is available for the Home page.

  2. On the Training page, click a video name to open and play the training video or click the View All Videos link to access all available FireLight training videos from the Training page. Following are some examples.

    • Click Navigation Bar on the upper right to open the FireLight Navigation Bar dialog and automatically start the Navigation Bar training video.

    • Click View All Videos to show all of the available FireLight training videos. Scroll down to view all of the available videos. Click a video name to open a dialog and automatically start the video.

  3. After clicking a video name, the training video opens in a dialog.

  4. On the lower left, click the pause and play buttons to stop and start the video.
  5. On the keyboard, press the down arrow and up arrow to lower and raise the volume on the video.
  6. When the video ends, click the button on the upper right of the dialog to close the dialog and return to the Training window.
  7. Close the Training window when finished.

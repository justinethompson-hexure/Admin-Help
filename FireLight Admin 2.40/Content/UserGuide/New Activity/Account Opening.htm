Account Opening [User Instructions]

Account Opening activity is available if your organization has implemented the Account Opening feature.

An Account Opening activity is a pre-sale activity that can be a launch point into other supported FireLight® activities. After submission of a new Account Opening, you cannot edit it.

NOTE: If you enter FireLight through your organization’s CRM system and FireLight launches directly into a new Account Opening, skip steps 1 through 4.

  1. On the Home page under Start New or on the New Activity menu, click Account Opening.  

  2. Select a Jurisdiction and a Custodian in the lists.

    NOTE: The Custodian list is not available unless more than one custodian exists.

  3. Click Create.

  4. Edit the Name of the account opening and click Create.

    OPTIONAL: If user sharing is enabled, select an option in the On Behalf of list.

  5. Fill in the fields on the new account form or wizard and click CONTINUE when Data Entry reaches 100%. See Data Entry Step [User Instructions] for detailed information.

  6. Complete the signing process and click CONTINUE. See Signatures Step [User Instructions] for detailed information. Simplified instructions follow.

    1. Click Use E-Signature to electronically sign the documents.
    2. In the List of Required Signers, click a signer type button (e.g., click Account Holder).
    3. Click Sign Now to sign on this device.
    4. Fill in the Identification Verification fields and click Verified.
    5. Account Holder reviews the documents, selects the checkbox for I have reviewed and agree with the terms expressed within this document, and clicks Sign.
    6. Account Holder enters their signing information (Signer Full Name and City) and clicks I Consent to complete their signature capture.
    7. In the List of Required Signers, click Agent. You review the documents and click Sign.
    8. Enter your Agent On-Site Signature information and click I Consent.
    9. Click CONTINUE.

  7. NOTE: Skip this step if your organization does not provide a pre-submission review. See Pre-Submission Review Step [User Instructions] for detailed information. Simplified instructions follow.

    1. Click an option on the action menu:

      • Submit to submit the account opening. Go to step 8.

      • Request Review to request a pre-submission review.

    2. After clicking Request Review, click a button to Send Request to Reviewer(s) or to Decline E-Review.

      If you decline e-review, click OK on the Electronic Review Declined dialog.

      NOTE: If your organization requires a pre-submission review, this page does not appear and you go directly to step c.

    3. Select the checkbox next to one or more names for Reviewer Name. Click Send Email Request. Click Back to App to return to the account opening.

    NOTE: The account opening could also go into a post-submission review if your organization requires it. Upon final reviewer approval, the account opening is automatically submitted.

  8. If no pre-submission review, click CONTINUE to submit the new account opening.

  9. After the account opening is submitted, click CONTINUE on the upper right and click an option on the action menu to begin a new activity linked to the account opening.

Subsequent Payment [User Instructions]

Subsequent Payment activity is available if your organization has implemented the Subsequent Payment feature.

NOTE: If you enter FireLight through your organization’s CRM system and FireLight® launches directly into a Subsequent Payment activity, skip steps 1 through 4.

  1. On the Home page under Start New or on the New Activity menu, click Subsequent Payment.

  2. Select a Jurisdiction and Product Type from the drop-down lists. In Product Type, select the All option to view a list of all available products. Click a Product Name.

    In the following example, select Annuity in Product Type and click the Subsequent Payment product.

  3. Click Create.

  4. If applicable, enter a Name for the Subsequent Payment and click Create.

  5. Enter the applicable information on the form. Required fields appear with a red background.

  6. When Data Entry reaches 100%, click CONTINUE to proceed. See the Data Entry Step [User Instructions] for detailed information about entering data in forms.

    (Optional) If your organization includes a pre-submission Review step, a submenu appears. Click Review to proceed to the Review process or click Submit to submit the subsequent premium payment.

  7. Click Yes on the Confirmation dialog.

  8. The following message appears when the subsequent payment is submitted.

Click the browser Back button to return to the previous topic.

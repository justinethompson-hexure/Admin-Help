Illustration [User Instructions]

Illustration activity is available if your organization has implemented the FireLight® Illustration feature.

NOTE: Illustration activity is available only in wizard format.

  1. On the Home page under Start New or on the New Activity menu, click Illustration.

    NOTE: If you enter FireLight through your organization’s CRM system and FireLight launches directly into an illustration, skip steps 1 through 4.

  2. On the Create New Illustration page, select a Jurisdiction and Product Type in the lists. In Product Type, select the All option to view a list of all available products. Click a Product Name.

    NOTE: If Localization is enabled for your organization, select an option in the Locale list if the locale is different from the default locale.

  3. Fill in the information on the illustration wizard.

    If the illustration has a schedule: above the schedule, select the duration type in the Durations use list and click the adjustment type (Amount or Percent) in the Adjustments use list. Inside the schedule, make the application selections and enter the amounts or percentages in the applicable columns. Click the button on the right of the schedule to add a row to the schedule. The following examples are for a premium schedule before and after adding a row.

    To delete a row, click the minus button on the right of the schedule.

    If a schedule validation error occurs, pause the mouse over the error icon on the left of the row to view the validation message.

    NOTE: If a validation error occurs in the illustration, an alert icon appears next to Alerts and a red balloon appears on the right of the toolbar. FireLight displays fields with validation errors in a red background.

    1. On the toolbar, click the alert icon on the left or click the red balloon on the right to display the validation message.

    2. Correct the data error in the field(s) with a red background.

    3. FireLight automatically closes the error message(s).

  4. On the toolbar, click Illustrate to view calculation results for the illustration in Quick View. On the lower right, click Close to return to the illustration.

  5. On the toolbar, click Print to generate and open the illustration reports in a separate tab. Pause the mouse over the PDF page to enable the PDF controls for sizing, viewing, saving, and printing. Close the web browser tab when finished.

  6. To save the illustration, click Save on the navigation bar, enter the Illustration Name in the Save Illustration dialog, and click Save. If you do not save the illustration, it will not be added to the Recent Activity list on the Home page or to the All Activities page.

    If you close an illustration without first saving it, a dialog opens. Click Yes to save the illustration or click No or Cancel to exit the dialog without saving changes.

  7. (Optional) If your organization has the Agent Illustration PDF Output option enabled and you are sales desk, assistant, etc., click the Send Email button next to the Print button on the illustration toolbar and fill in the information on the Send Email dialog. If a “|CLIENT_NAME|” tag appears in the Subject and Message, replace it with the client’s name. Click Send to send an email to the agent with a link to the illustration PDF output. Repeat for each agent to receive the email. This allows the agent to click the link within the email and quickly print the illustration PDF output. Audit entries are recorded each time the email is sent, when the agent clicks the link in the email, and if the email is canceled.

  8. On the Other Actions menu, click Sent Emails to open a dialog and review or cancel Agent Illustration PDF Output emails sent to the agent. To cancel the email, click the Cancel link in the Sent Email dialog and click OK on the confirmation dialog. The email is canceled and no email is sent to the agent.

    Other Actions menu before and after saving the illustration (with Share and Transfer enabled).

    The agent receives an email similar to the following and clicks the link to open a web browser.

    The illustration PDF output opens in a web browser window where the agent can view and print the illustration output. When finished, the agent closes the web browser window.

  9. If your organization has the Application activity enabled in FireLight, click Apply (or whatever your organization named the continue button) on the right of the toolbar to begin a new application for the illustrated product. FireLight pre-fills data entered on the product illustration into matching fields on the product application and saves the illustration PDF output. Go to Application.

  10. To make changes to an illustration after you have created the application, but before the application is locked, open the illustration and change the applicable data. Once complete, click Update on the right of the toolbar. FireLight passes the updated data into the matching fields on the product Application.

Submit the Activity [User Instructions]

NOTE: If your organization includes a post-submission (OSJ) review step and auto submit is enabled, upon reviewer approval FireLight® automatically submits an activity order to the back office for processing and a dimmed FINISHED button appears on the right.

If your organization does not have auto submit enabled, after the signature step the next step is for the Agent or Representative to submit the activity package for processing.

  1. Complete the Signatures step.
  2. On the right, click the CONTINUE button to proceed with activity submission.

  3. On the Confirmation dialog, click OK to submit the activity. Click Cancel to return to the activity in FireLight.

    NOTE: If the signature field enforces state match and signature capture is outside of the solicitation state of the activity, an Invalid Signatures dialog appears. Click Submit to submit the activity. Click Cancel to cancel activity submission. If you click Submit, FireLight records an audit that the agent overrode the signer state validation.

  4. If your organization enabled the optional post-submission Review Queue feature, a notification informs you that the activity is pending review.

    When the reviewer approves the activity, FireLight notifies the Agent with a FireLight message and via email if you select Send Message Center Emails on the Preferences [User Instructions] page.

    If your organization did not enable the optional Review Queue feature, a notification informs you that the activity is pending transmission. This is a fully configurable message by organization.

    NOTE: If your organization enabled the "HTTP Redirect on Submit" option in FireLight Admin, you are redirected to an external web page after clicking the Submit button and are no longer in FireLight.

  5. To perform other tasks in FireLight, do the following:

    • Click the Other Actions link to open the Other Actions menu and perform other tasks in FireLight, such as printing the application documents, etc. See Other Actions Menu [User Instructions].

    • Click the Close link to close the notification and return to the application package.

  6. After successful activity package submission:

    1. FireLight displays a dimmed FINISHED button on the far right.

    2. FireLight sends an email notification to all of the signers except for the Agent. The email wording is configurable by the organization.

    3. The client clicks the link in the email, enters their Last 4 Digits of SSN/Government ID and Birth Date in the FireLight Welcome dialog, and clicks Enter.

    4. In the View/Download PDF Documents dialog, the client clicks a link to View All or Download All activity documents or to View or Download a single document. The View All and Download All options display all of the documents combined into one PDF file.

    5. Client clicks the Log Off button to log off FireLight.

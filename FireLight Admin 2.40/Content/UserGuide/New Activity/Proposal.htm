  6. Enter Solve Data

  7. Enter your solve data in the right hand panel and click "Apply Changes". Don’t worry, you can quickly adjust these values later if needed.

  8. Select Products

  9. Your originally selected products will be filtered down to match the solve scenario entered. From there, pick the products you want to include in the proposal.

  10. Add Pages

  11. Once you’ve selected products, click the “Page Library” button to start adding views. You can add as many pages as you’d like, and edit or remove them as needed.

  12. Save & Download

  13. Need to finish up later? Save the proposal and come back to it later. Ready to deliver the proposal? Click on the download button to save PDF to your local device and share.

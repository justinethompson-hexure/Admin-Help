Milliman Automated Underwriting

Each insurer can tailor Milliman to address their unique underwriting guidelines.

Following is a sample wizard experience for using Milliman automated underwriting, which allows agents to perform quality real-time risk assessment on individual life insurance applicants while simultaneously completing a FireLight application.

  1. Fill in the Insured information.

    If MIB is included as part of the order, the wizard includes additional required fields for Resident Country, Birth Country, Birth State (of the specific birth country), and Occupation.

    If MVR is included as part of the order, the wizard includes additional required fields for Driver's License and Driver's License State.

    Milliman needs to know how the authorization to retrieve this information was signed. If it not electronically signed, select the Wet Signed or Oral voice signature option. Next, click the Upload Copy of Signed Authorization button to open the Documents Documents [User Instructions] dialog and upload a copy of the signed authorization document so it can be attached to the order.

  2. The carrier determines which fields are visible on the Milliman wizard page. In this example, the carrier has four underwriting Risk Classifications.

    Click the Milliman Request custom action button. A notification states a back office action is being performed. Wait for the Milliman results. FireLight polls Milliman every 15 seconds to return those responses.

  3. Milliman returns an underwriting decision for each risk category based on who they are calling based on prescription Rx information as well as MIB and/or MVR depending on the carrier's preferences.

    Milliman's basic underwriting classifications are:

    10 = Accept / Approved
    20 = Review
    30 = Decline

  4. Carrier-specific data can be included on an additional Milliman Return Data wizard page that may or may not be visible to the agent.

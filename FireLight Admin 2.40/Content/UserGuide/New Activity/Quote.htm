Quote [User Instructions]

Quote activity is available if your organization has implemented the Quote activity. The Quote activity currently provides quotes for Life.

NOTE: Quote activity is available only in wizard format. Quote for Annuity products is not yet fully functional.

Create a Quote Proposal for Life Products

  1. On the Home page under Start New or on the New Activity menu, click Quote to open the Quote wizard.

  2. On the Quote toolbar, click Life.

    NOTE: The red bubble icon for Required Fields does not show on the upper right in the IXN Quote activity. All required fields are handled on an individual level within the quoter.

  3. In the left column Proposed Insured section, enter the information for the proposed insured.

    • First Name

    • Last Name

    • Date of Birth or Age

    • Gender

    • Tobacco Use

    • State

    • Health Options - The number of health options may need to be reduced if other options are selected. For example, you cannot select all health options with all carriers, but all health options can be selected with a single carrier.

  4. In the center column Product section, select the product types (Term Periods, To Age, Term ROP, Whole Life, and Final Expense) and carriers to filter the quotes by product and carrier. Up to five product types can be selected when the user selects all carriers. When the number of carriers is limited, more than five product types can be selected.

  5. In the right column Coverage and Agent sections, enter or select the coverage and agent information. Up to three Face Amounts can be entered.

    Optionally, click the Calculate Needs link to calculate the face amount based on the Debts and Expenses, Income and Assets. See "Needs Analysis Calculator" below.

  6. On the lower right of the wizard, click Run Quote.

    The Quote Results contain the client information, Coverage, and Agent information followed by the number of results and a search field. Quote results display on separate tabs for each term period duration.

    Click Show Details at the bottom of a product quote to show product details. Click a tab to show product Features, Forms, Carrier Overview, Premiums, and Important Information on separate tabs.

    Click Hide Details to hide product details.

  7. (Optional) On the upper right of the quote, click the button to the right of the search field to expand the dialog. Enter a Carrier name if applicable. Select search options to Sort By, Group By, Filter By, and select Product Features to filter the quote results.

  8. On one or more tabs for the desired term period, select the checkbox for one or more product quotes. The Share and Compare buttons become available.

  9. Click the Compare button to open the Comparing Quotes dialog. Use the scroll bar to scroll left and right through the columns of the comparison. On the lower right of the comparison, click the Share button to select a delivery method and share the Quote with the client.

  10. Click the Share button above the product selection or click the Share button on the Comparing Quotes dialog to open the Select a Delivery Method dialog.

    On the Select a Delivery Method dialog, click a Download, Email or Text button for the delivery method and click the Next button. Download is the default.

    On the Select a PDF Layout dialog, click a PDF layout for the quotes: Detailed Comparison, Condensed Premium Comparison, Premium Comparison, or Feature Comparison and click the Next button.

    The selected Delivery Method determines the contents of the Enter Information dialog.

    Download delivery method:

    On the Enter Information dialog, enter Agent Information and ensure Recipient Information is correct. Click the Download button.

    Email delivery method:

    On the Enter Information dialog, enter Agent Information and Recipient Information and click the Send button. The client receives an email with the attached quote PDF file.

    Text delivery method:

    On the Enter Information dialog, enter Agent Information, enter Recipient Information, and click the Send button.

    On the Success dialog, click View PDF to open and view the quote PDF document.

    Click the X on the upper right to close the dialog.

  11. (Optional) Save the quote: on the navigation bar, click Save. In the Save Quote dialog, enter a Name for the quote and click Save. Save the quote under a new name: in the Other Actions menu on the navigation bar, click Copy Activity. In the Copy Activity dialog, enter a new name for the quote and click OK.

    After saving a quote, the Share and Transfer options become available on the Other Actions menu. See Share [User Instructions] and Transfer [User Instructions] topics for instructions.

  12. On the Quote page, to the right of the Quote, pause the mouse (hover) over the premium amounts of a quote and click the Get Started button.

    On the Pick An Action dialog, click one of the following options:

    • Apply – begins an application in FireLight (if E-Application is enabled)

    • E-Ticket – begins an E-Ticket in FireLight (if E-Ticket is enabled)

    • Illustrate – begins an illustration in FireLight (if Illustration is enabled)

    • Download PDF – download PDF delivery method

    • Text PDF – text PDF delivery method

    • Email PDF – email PDF delivery method

    Complete the application, e-ticket, or illustration in FireLight.

    • Illustration: complete the illustration and click the Apply button to create an application.

    • Application: create an application. When data entry is completed, click Continue to move to the next step in the process until signatures and any applicable review are complete.

    • E-Ticket: complete the Quote Ticket App. When data entry is completed, click Continue to move to signatures.

    NOTE: In the application or e-ticket on the Other Actions menu, click Display/Print PDF, select the documents to print including the Quote, and click Print Selected Documents to create a PDF file with the selected Application or E-Ticket and quote documents.

  13. On the upper right of the Life Quote page, click the New Quote button to start a new quote.

Needs Analysis Calculator

Use the Needs Analysis Calculator to calculate the face amount based on the Debts and Expenses, Income, and Assets.

  1. In the Coverage section on the upper right, click the Calculate Needs link.

    1. Fill in the information and click Calculate Needs to display the Estimated Coverage Need.

      Click Back to Needs Analysis to return to the Needs Analysis Calculator and make changes.

    2. Click Quote With This Coverage to return to the Life Quote page with the Estimated Coverage Need added to the Face Amounts. Click the X on the right of a Face Amount to delete it from the quote.

Toolbox

Use the Toolbox to access Underwriting Guidelines

  1. On the lower left of the Life Quote page, click the Toolbox button to open the Toolbox.

  2. Click a button to do the following:

    • Click the Underwriting Guides button to open a list of available underwriting guides. Click a link to view the underwriting guide. Click Back To Tools to return to the Toolbox.

    • Click the Quoter Tour button to take a tour of the Life Quoter. Click Start Tour to begin the tour. (Optionally, click Maybe Later to close the Toolbox and return to the Life Quote page.)

      Click Next on each succeeding dialog to work through the tour.

      When the tour completes, click the Close button on the Tour Completed dialog to return to the Life Quote page.

    • Click the Needs Analysis button open the Needs Analysis Calculator as shown above.

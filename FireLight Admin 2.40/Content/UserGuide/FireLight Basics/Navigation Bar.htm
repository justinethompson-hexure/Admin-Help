Navigation Bar [User Instructions]

Buttons on the top navigation bar enable you to navigate to other areas of the system or to Log Off.

If your organization enabled Training, the top navigation bar includes a Training button. See the Training section for more information.

If your organization enabled the multiple open activities feature, the top navigation bar includes an Open Activities (#) menu. The number next to the menu name indicates the number of open activities. Select an activity in the list to navigate among the open activities. See Multiple Open Activities for more information on how to use this feature.

On the left of the navigation bar, click the FireLight logo to open the About dialog and view Version, Copyright, and Open Source License Info.

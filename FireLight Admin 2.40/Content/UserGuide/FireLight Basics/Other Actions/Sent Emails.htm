Sent Emails [User Instructions]

NOTE: The Sent Emails option appears for Illustration activities only.

  1. On the Other Actions menu, click Sent Emails to open a dialog and review or cancel Agent Illustration PDF Output emails sent to the agent.

    To cancel the email, click the Cancel link in the Sent Email dialog and click OK on the confirmation dialog. The email is cancelled and no email is sent to the agent.

  2. The agent receives an email similar to the following and clicks the link to open a web browser.

  3. The illustration PDF output opens in a web browser window where the agent can view and print the illustration output. When finished, the agent closes the web browser window.

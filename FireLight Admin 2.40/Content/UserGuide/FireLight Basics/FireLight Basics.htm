FireLight Basics [User Instructions]

Use FireLight® to start and manage new activities (accounts, applications, illustrations, need determinations, quotes, etc.), capture signatures, submit activities to the back office for processing, enter data, and request reviews as applicable for your organization.

The information in this section provides instructions for getting started with using the FireLight® web platform (FireLight).

NOTE: The FireLight images may look slightly different depending on your organization’s branding choices, optional features, and the web browser you are using.

Step through the following to get started quickly.

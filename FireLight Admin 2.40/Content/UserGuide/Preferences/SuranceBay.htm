SuranceBay

If your group admin has enabled SuranceBay, the Preferences page includes a SuranceBay section is available on the Preferences page.

Test Credentials

Follow these steps to test your SuranceBay credentials.

  1. Type your User Name in the text box.

  2. Type your password in the text box.

  3. Click Test.

 

The credentials you enter here are used to automatically log you in to the SureLC portal when appropriate.

Note: Can-Sell services are applicable only to life and annuity insurance products.

Initiate Application Activity in Firelight

User Experience 1: Valid Credentials

Provided your credentials are valid, follow the steps below to initiate an application in FireLight.

Navigate to FIreLight Applications, and then click Applications.

A page with a Producer Profile retrieved from SureLC appears and displays a list of available states and details related to licensing, training, and appointments.

 

Select a Jurisdiction, Locale, and Product Type.

A Can Sell widget appears for eligible products within the selected jurisdiction.

If Product is not mapped in the SureLC side, the Can Sell Widget displays the following.

 

Select the widget associated with the desired product.

Firelight displays SureLC Application Widget with Producer Requirements. For the below image, the producer has missing requirements in selected state.

Close the widget or click Fix Issues to attempt to fix the product issues.

 

If you clicked Fix Issues, the SureLC application appears.

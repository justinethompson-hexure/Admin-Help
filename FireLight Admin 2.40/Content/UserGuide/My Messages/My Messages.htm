My Messages [User Instructions]

NOTE: If you select the Send Message Center Emails checkbox in Preferences [User Instructions], FireLight® sends emails to your email address for any messages posted in the FireLight message center. This includes email failure notifications from the SendGrid email delivery system.

From anywhere in FireLight, click the message button on the upper right of the Home page or on the right of the navigation bar to go to My Messages and view activity related messages sent and received from the FireLight message center.

In the following image, the number within the red background indicates the number of new messages that have not been viewed.

When new messages exist for an activity, My Messages list displays the associated activity messages in an expanded view as shown below.

Do any of the following:

  • Click the down double arrows to expand an activity name and access messages that FireLight sent or received. Click the up double arrows to collapse the message view.

  • Click the Sent button to expand and read a message sent from FireLight. When finished, click the Sent button again to collapse the message.

    NOTE: You can click a link within the message to Resend this message, Send new email to [Client Name], or Send Passcode to [Client Name]’s Cell Phone.

  • Click the Inbox button to expand and read a message that FireLight sent.

  • Click the Back button on the navigation bar to return to the previous page in FireLight.

  • Click Log Off on the navigation bar to log off FireLight.

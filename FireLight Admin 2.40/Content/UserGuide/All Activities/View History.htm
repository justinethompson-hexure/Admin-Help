View History [User Instructions]

NOTE: See the FireLight Audit SummaryFireLight Audit Summary document in the Admin Help for detailed information about audits.

  1. In the Last Audit Entry section of an activity, click the View History link to view audit history for the activity in the History dialog.

  2. Click on the upper right to close the dialog.

    NOTE: If your organization has post submission polling services enabled, a separate History dialog displays only the post submission audit history. Any references to the Provider are listed as "Back Office."

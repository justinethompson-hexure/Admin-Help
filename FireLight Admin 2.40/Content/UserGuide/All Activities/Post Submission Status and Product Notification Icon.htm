Post-Submission Status and Product Notification Icon

Post-Submission Status Notification

For organizations that have post-submission polling services set up in the Provider, when a polling service occurs and a notification is received for an activity, a post-submission status “envelope” icon appears on the right of the activity buttons as shown below. Click the icon to go to the message center and review the post-submission status message.

The post-submission message contains the agent name, case name, status, and carrier along with any Provider specific information, such as instructions for the agent. Post-submission statuses include Carrier Review Complete, Approved (Carrier status), Declined (Carrier status), Further Review (Carrier status), and In Carrier Review. Once you open a post-submission status message, the envelope icon changes to , which indicates that no unacknowledged post-submission messages exist for the activity.

Product Notification

In the All Activities list, for products that have a product notification set up in FireLight® Admin, when a product notification message is received for an activity, a notification “envelope” icon appears for the activity on the right of the activity buttons as shown below. Click the icon to go to the message center and review the product notification message.

Product notifications include a product specific message, for example, an upcoming rate change or product expiration message. Once you open a product notification message, the envelope icon changes to , which indicates that no unacknowledged product notification messages exist for the activity.

Delete

In an activity, click the Delete button and then click OK on the Confirm Delete dialog to delete the activity from the All Activities page and from the Recent Activity list on the Home page. Click Cancel to cancel the deletion.

NOTES: FireLightÂ® does not allow deletion of a Needs Determination until after deletion of the associated application. FireLight does not allow deletion of activities from All Activities or from the Recent Activity grid on the Home page that have a LexisNexis Client ID or Risk Classifier custom action button that has been clicked. The Delete button is not available for those activities in All Activities.

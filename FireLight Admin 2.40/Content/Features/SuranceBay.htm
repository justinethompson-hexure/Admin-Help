SuranceBay

If your group admin has enabled SuranceBay, the Preferences page includes a SuranceBay section, within which you can enter and test your SuranceBay credentials. The credentials you enter here are used to automatically log you in to the SureLC portal when appropriate.

Note: Can-Sell services are applicable only to life and annuity insurance products.

Test Credentials

Follow these steps to test your SuranceBay credentials.

  1. Type your User Name in the text box.

  2. Type your password in the text box.

  3. Click Test.

Initiate Application Activity in Firelight

After you have entered valid credentials, follow the steps below to initiate an application in FireLight.

  1. Navigate to FireLight Applications, and then click Applications.

    2. A page with a Producer Profile retrieved from SureLC appears and displays a list of available states and details related to licensing, training, and appointments.

  2. Select a Jurisdiction and Locale.

    3. A Can Sell widget appears for eligible products within the selected jurisdiction.

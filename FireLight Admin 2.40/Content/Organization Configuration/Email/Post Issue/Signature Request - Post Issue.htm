Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags and Notes

Action

Sends a request to the client to e-sign the post issue activity

Template Name: Signature Request - Post Issue1

Email Subject Line: <CASE_NAME> - Please complete your signature

Message:

Dear <CLIENT_NAME>,

Thank you for providing your client information.

Use the link included at the bottom of the email to start the signing process. You is asked to acknowledge your acceptance of the disclosure terms and consents. The instruction for completing your 'Electronic Signature' is provided as well.

Please use your birth date and the last 4 digits of your SSN to login.

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me.

If you need help signing, please watch this short guide: https://vimeopro.com/user47344059/firelight-training/video/263943487

Post Issue

Sincerely,

<AGENT_NAME>

To sign your client information, click on <URL_LINK>, enter the last 4 digits of your SSN, and your birth date. If a new window does not automatically appear, you may have to copy the link and paste it into the address bar of a new browser window.

This template is available for sending when the Allow E-Signature checkbox is selected in Organization Configuration/Organization Settings page on the Signature tab.

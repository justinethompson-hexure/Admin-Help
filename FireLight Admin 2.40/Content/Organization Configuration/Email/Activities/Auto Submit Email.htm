Auto Submit Email
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Notifies the agent when an application is automatically submitted when signatures are complete.

Template Name: Auto Submit Email Notification

Email Subject Line: Automatic Submission Notification

Message:

Dear <AGENT_NAME>,

The application '<APPLICATION_NAME>' has been automatically submitted to the carrier.

This email is available for sending when the Enable Auto Submit and Enable Auto Submit Email Notification checkboxes are selected in Organization Configuration/Groups on the App tab.

Agent Name/Email used in original request

From Name / Email

Organization Name/Email

No cc Sender

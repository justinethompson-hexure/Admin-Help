Request Client to Fill Activity
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Sends a request to the client to complete their electronic application (agent selects "Request Client to Fill App" from "Other Actions" menu and also selects "Send Email Request")

Template Name: Request Client to Fill Activity1

Email Subject Line: <APPLICATION_NAME> - Please complete filling your application

Message:

Dear <CLIENT_NAME>,

Thank you for applying for '<PRODUCT_NAME>' of <COMPANY_NAME>.

To complete your electronic application, please click on the link at the bottom of the email. You is asked to acknowledge your acceptance of the disclosure terms and consents.

Please use your birth date and the last 4 digits of your SSN to login.

If you have questions please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely,

<AGENT_NAME>

To view and complete your application, click on <URL_LINK>, enter the last 4 digits of your SSN, and birth date. If a new window does not automatically appear, you may have to copy the link and paste it into the address bar of new browser window.

This template is available for sending when the Allow Client E-Fill checkbox is selected in Organization Configuration/Organization Settings on the Activity tab.

To Name / Email

Client Name/Email typed in email fields or pre-filled from mapped dataitems

From Name / Email

Default: Session User’s Preference Name/ Email2

Use Agent: Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

Use DMARC1: Organization's Support Email

CC Address

If Enable Cc Sender In All Requests is set in Organization Configuration/Groups page on the App tab, cc email sent to ‘From Email’ address

Tele-Sign Document Delivery
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Email sent to the client to request review and signing of the Tele-Sign application documents1

Template Name: Tele-Sign Document Delivery

Email Subject Line: <APPLICATION_NAME> - Please Review Your Application

Message:

Dear <CLIENT_NAME>,

Use the link included at the bottom of the email to view the documents associated with your application.

Please use your birth date and the last 4 digits of your SSN to login.

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely,

<AGENT_NAME>

To sign your application, click on <URL_LINK> and enter the last 4 digits of your SSN and your birth date. If a new window does not automatically appear, you may have to copy the link and paste it into the address bar of a new browser window.

This template is available when the Enable Tele-Sign and Enable Tele-Sign Email Link checkboxes are selected in Organization Configuration/Groups on the App tab. Also, a Tele-Sign script must be added to the Organization Configuration/Tele-Sign Scripts page for the product, and the product Tele-Sign Script must be added to the Options tab for the product in the Sales Agreements in Product Configuration.

To Name / Email

Client Name/Email typed in email fields or pre-filled from mapped dataitems

From Name / Email

Default: Session User’s Preference Name/ Email2

Use Agent: Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

Use DMARC1: Organization's Support Email

CC Address

If Enable Cc Sender In All Requests is set in Organization Configuration/Groups page on the App tab, cc email sent to ‘From Email’ address

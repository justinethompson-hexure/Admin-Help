Passcode Notification
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

The Passcode Notificaton email template generates a unique passcode for the specific activity. It is sent in several scenarios within the system, for example: when the advisor selects the Request Client to Fill App or Request Client to Fill & Sign option on the Other Actions menu, when the advisor selects the Send Email Request action during the signing ceremony, when the advisor submits the Client activity/application for review, etc.

Template Name: Passcode Notification

Email Subject Line: Passcode Application

Message:

The Passcode For the Recent Request is <PASSCODE> for <APPLICATION_NAME>, <APPLICATION_ID>, <DATE_TIME>, <PRODUCT_NAME>, <PRODUCT_TYPE>, <COMPANY_NAME>.

This email is available for sending when the Allow PassCode Email option is selected in Organization Configuration/Groups page on the App tab in the Email section.

Addressee receives the email based on information used in the request.

Auto Submit E-Delivery Email
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Notifies the agent when the E-Delivery process has completed and the activity has been automatically submitted.

Template Name: Auto Submit E-Delivery Email

Email Subject Line: Automatic E-Delivery Submission Notification

Message:

Dear <AGENT_NAME>,

The E-Delivery process has completed and '<APPLICATION_NAME>' has been automatically submitted.

This email is available for sending when the Enable Auto Submit and Enable Auto Submit Email Notification checkboxes are selected in Organization Configuration/Groups on the App tab and the E-Delivery checkbox is selected in Groups on the Activity Access tab.

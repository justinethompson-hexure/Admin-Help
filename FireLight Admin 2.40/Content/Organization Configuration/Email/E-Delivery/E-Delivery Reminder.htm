E-Delivery Reminder

E-Delivery Reminder
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Sends a reminder to the Agent of pending E-Delivery activities that are ready for completion.

Template Name: E-Delivery Reminder

Email Subject Line: E-Delivery Submission Reminder

Message:

Dear <AGENT_NAME>,

The following E-Delivery transactions are ready for completion:

<MESSAGE>

This email is available for sending when E-Delivery Reminder Frequency is set to "Daily" or "Weekly" in Organization Configuration/Organization Settings on the Activity tab. "Daily" frequency emails go out at 6:00 AM UTC. "Weekly" frequency emails go out on Sunday at 7 AM UTC.

To Name / Email

Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

From Name / Email

Default: Session User’s Preference Name/ Email2

Use Agent: Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

Use DMARC1: Organization's Support Email

CC Address

No cc Sender

Available Tags3

<AGENT_NAME>
<MESSAGE>6
<STATUS>
<TRANSACTION_TYPE>
ApplicationSearchItem4 (e.g., add <CLIENT_NAME>)

Notes:

6 The <MESSAGE> tag will populate the email with the list of E-Delivery activities ready to be submitted. It can also include the Client Name, processing instruction (Agent Directed, Client Directed), and case status (parent case), e.g., Pending Client Review, Pending Agent Review, etc., per client request.

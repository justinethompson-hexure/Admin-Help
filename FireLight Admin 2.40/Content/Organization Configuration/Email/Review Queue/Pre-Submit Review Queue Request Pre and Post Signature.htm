Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Requests reviewer to perform review prior to submission of activity1

Template Name: Pre-Submit Review Queue Request (Pre and Post Signature)

Email Subject Line: Please review the following application

Message:

Dear <REVIEWER_NAME>,

Please review the following client's application for '<PRODUCT_NAME>' of <COMPANY_NAME>.

Click on the link at the bottom of the email to review and approve the application. You is asked to acknowledge your acceptance of the disclosure terms and consents. The instruction for completing your 'Electronic Signature' is provided as well.

Please use the Passcode to login. I is providing this Passcode via phone or a separate email.

If you have questions please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely,

<AGENT_NAME>

To review the application, click on <URL_LINK>, and enter the Passcode. If a new window does not automatically appear, you may have to copy the link and paste it into the address bar of new browser window.

This template is available for sending when the Allow Pre-Signature Review or Allow Post-Signature Review checkbox is selected in Product Configuration/on the Activities page for each activity type that includes pre-submission review. Be sure to set the reviewer access options in Organization Configuration/Groups page, Review Queue tab.

To Name / Email

Reviewer Name/Email typed in email fields or reviewer Name/Email info saved in session user’s preferences

From Name / Email

Default: Session User’s Preference Name/ Email2

Use Agent: Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

Use DMARC1: Organization's Support Email

CC Address

If Enable Cc Sender In All Requests is set in Groups on the App tab, cc email sent to ‘From Email’ address

E-Ticket Next Steps Notification

E-Ticket Next Steps Notification
Action, To Name/Email, From Name/Email, CC Address, Available Tags, and Notes

Action

Notifies the client about the next steps after applying for a life product via the IXN Life Quote E-Ticket. This email template is triggered and sent to the Insured once the agent has completed the E-Ticket, selected "Continue" and selects "Yes" on the Confirmation dialog that is displayed.

Template Name: E-Ticket Next Steps Notification1

Email Subject Line: <CLIENT_NAME> Regarding <APPLICATION_NAME> - Next steps

Message:

Dear <CLIENT_NAME>,

Thank you for applying for ‘<PRODUCT_NAME>’ of <COMPANY_NAME>. You is contacted by a trained professional <MESSAGE> to conduct an interview in order to complete the next phase of your application process. Having the following information available will help you keep your interview as brief as possible:

<MESSAGE>

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely, <AGENT_NAME>

NOTES:

  • The first instance of <MESSAGE> within the body of the email is customized by the client to indicate the vendor serving as the fulfillment center on behalf of the carrier.

  • The final instance of <MESSAGE> can be customized by the client to state next steps.

This template is available for sending when the Ticket App checkbox is selected in Organization Configuration/Groups page on the Activity Access tab.

See Also:

To Name / Email

Client Name/Email typed in email fields or pre-filled from mapped dataitems. Email is to be sent to the email address associated with the dataitemid "Insured_Primary_EmailAddress" that is in the ticket template wizard. Email will not be sent if there are no values for Insured_Primary_EmailAddress and Insured_FullName.

From Name / Email

Default: Session User’s Preference Name/ Email2

Use Agent: Owning Agent’s Preference Name/Email

Use DMARC1: Organization's Support Email

CC Address

If Enable Cc Sender In All Requests is set in Organization Configuration/Groups page on the App tab, cc email sent to ‘From Email’ address

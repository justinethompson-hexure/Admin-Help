History

On the Organization page, view the history of any changes made on the Organization Settings page by clicking the History tab.

The History tab displays the following.

Action shows the type of update made on the Organization Settings page, including the text box, dropdown, or checkbox updated and what specific change was made.

Old Value shows the value of the text box, dropdown, or checkbox prior to the action.

New Value shows the value of the text box, dropdown, or checkbox after the action.

Action Date shows the timestamp associated with the action.

Login shows the login ID of the person who updated the text box, dropdown, or checkbox.

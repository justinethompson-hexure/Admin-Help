Copy Groups

Use the Groups page to copy groups for your organization.

  1. In the grid at the top of the Groups page, select the group to be copied.
  2. At the bottom of the page, click Copy Group.
  3. On the Select Groups Settings to Copy window that appears, enter a Name, Illustration Services Role Code, TC Role Codes, and Firm Org.

  4. Select the desired Copy All Settings for checkboxes (App, Activity Access, Needs Filter, Admin, Product Access, Debugging Tools, Review Queue, Case Access, Training).

  5. At the bottom of the window, click Save.

  6. At the bottom of the Groups page, click Save.
